About Me

I'm Franklyn Eziofor, a passionate Frontend Developer based in Awka, Anambra State, Nigeria. With a background in Physics, I bring analytical thinking and precision to every line of code I write.

I specialize in transforming design concepts into fast, responsive, and accessible web applications using modern technologies. Whether it's building elegant user interfaces or optimizing performance, I’m driven by the challenge of creating digital products that users genuinely enjoy interacting with.
